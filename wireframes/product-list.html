Product List Notes

  • Header: Dynamically displays the name of the category or search query.
  • Filters/Sort: Essential controls for refining the product list. Tapping 'Filters' would open a modal or new screen.
  • Product Grid: A 2-column grid is standard for mobile e-commerce, balancing image size with the number of visible items.
  • Product Card: Contains the essential information: image, name, and price. Tapping it navigates to the Product Detail screen.
  • Lazy Loading: As the user scrolls, more products would be loaded automatically (infinite scroll).
