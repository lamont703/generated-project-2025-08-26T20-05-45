Shopping Cart Notes

  • Item List: Each item clearly shows image, name, price, and quantity controls.
  • Quantity Controls: Users can easily adjust quantities or remove items directly from the cart.
  • Order Summary: A clear breakdown of costs is essential for user trust.
  • Checkout Button: The primary CTA, leading the user to the next step of the purchase flow.
  • Empty State: If the cart is empty, a message and a 'Start Shopping' button should be displayed (not shown here).
